Our Founder

Maj. Sudhanshu Shekhar Pandey, as the founder of SS Pandey & Associates, a prominent law firm in India, has gained recognition from peers, clients, and various international legal publications for his exceptional legal acumen and expertise. He is widely regarded as an astute lawyer and is acknowledged as one of the leading lawyers in India. Through his experience and knowledge, he has contributed to the legal profession and his firm's standing in the industry.

Maj. Sudhanshu Shekhar Pandey is a highly experienced and respected legal professional with a background in the Indian Army's Judge Advocate General's Department. After serving as a commissioned officer in the Army, he voluntarily left in the rank of Major in 2009 and has since been practicing law as a registered advocate with the Bar Council of Delhi.

Notably, Mr. Pandey has been actively advocating for the rights of serving soldiers, officers, disabled soldiers, war widows, and other ex-servicemen. His efforts have led to policy changes within the Armed Forces that have benefited thousands of individuals and their dependent family members. He is known for his persuasive skills, go-getter attitude, ingenuity in framing legal issues, and impressive arguments, earning him respect from his clients, peers, and the judiciary, including the Hon'ble Supreme Court.
