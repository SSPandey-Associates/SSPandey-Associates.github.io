Our Areas of Practice

Military Law

Military law is a system of laws that applies to members of the military. It is designed to maintain discipline and order in the armed forces, and to punish those who break the law. Military law is separate from civilian law, and it is enforced by military courts.

Some of the key areas of military law include:

  • Disciplinary law
  • Criminal law
  • Administrative law
  • Procedural law

Military law is a complex and specialized area of law. If you are facing a military law issue, it is important to seek the advice of an experienced military lawyer.

Service Laws

Service laws are a set of laws that govern the employment of people in the armed forces, civil services, and other government agencies. They cover a wide range of topics, including:

  • Recruitment and appointment
  • Terms and conditions of service
  • Discipline
  • Leave and pensions
  • Termination of employment

Service laws are complex and specialized, and it is important to seek the advice of an experienced lawyer if you have any questions about your rights or obligations under these laws.
