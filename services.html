Our Areas of Practice

Military Law

SS Pandey and Associates' military practice specializes in providing transactional and legal advisory services for day-to-day matters, defense issues, and policy governance. With an experience of serving as a JAG Officer in the Indian Army, Retd. Major SS Pandey has contributed to the field of Military Law with landmark judgments like the Sukhna Land Scam Case. Our expertise covers legal concerns related to remuneration, pension, and retirement benefits. We assist clients with various tenure-based aspects including commission, appointment, enrolment, probation, seniority, superannuation, service termination, disciplinary proceedings, and Court Martial. If you are facing a military law issue, it is important to seek the advice of an experienced military lawyer.
